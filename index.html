🗓 Track HistoryLas Vegas Motor Speedway opened in 1964. The first NASCAR Cup race was held in November 1988, won by Alan Kulwicki with his "Polish Victory Lap."
🏁 Track ShapePhoenix is a unique Tri-oval, 1.5 miles long. Turns 1–2 are flat; Turns 3–4 have 11° of banking. It runs clockwise — opposite most ovals.
☀️ Desert FactorAt 1,083 ft elevation, Phoenix's thin air reduces drag — cars run faster than the track size suggests. Tire wear is severe on the abrasive asphalt.
