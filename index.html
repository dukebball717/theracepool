🏆 Last Year's WinnerChristopher Bell won the 2025 spring race at Phoenix, his second straight spring win here. He led 159 of 312 laps.
👑 All-Time Wins LeaderKevin Harvick holds the record with 9 Cup wins at Phoenix — more than any driver in track history. He won here in 2001, '07, '10, '11, '13, '14, '15, '18, and '20.
📍 Distance from CharlottePhoenix Raceway is approximately 1,750 miles from Charlotte, NC — about a 25-hour drive or 4-hour flight west.
🗓 Track HistoryPhoenix Raceway opened in 1964. The first NASCAR Cup race was held in November 1988, won by Alan Kulwicki with his famous "Polish Victory Lap."
🏁 Track ShapePhoenix is a unique D-shaped oval, 1.0 mile long. Turns 1–2 are flat and wide; Turns 3–4 are tighter with 11° of banking. The track runs clockwise — opposite most ovals.
☀️ Desert FactorAt 1,083 ft elevation in the Sonoran Desert, Phoenix's thin air reduces aerodynamic drag — cars run faster here than the track size suggests. Tire wear is severe on the abrasive asphalt.
